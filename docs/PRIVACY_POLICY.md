# Privacy Policy — Cosmic Ocean

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-05  

Cosmic Ocean is a local-first Android app. Your tasks, wallpaper data, and settings are stored **only on your device**.

## Data We Collect
- **We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.**
- The app does not require an account.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## Data Stored On-Device
The app stores the following locally on your device to function:
- Tasks and task metadata (due dates, priority, context)
- Wallpaper settings and preferences
- Privacy settings

## Permissions
Cosmic Ocean may request device permissions to provide core features:
- **SET_WALLPAPER**: Apply wallpaper updates
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE**: Maintain real-time wallpaper updates
- **VIBRATE**: Optional haptics for due tasks
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: Optional reminders
- **INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: Reserved for future functionality; local-only behavior is enforced in the current release

## Data Security
All data remains on the device. You can remove your data at any time by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

## Children’s Privacy
Cosmic Ocean does not target children and does not knowingly collect data from children.
