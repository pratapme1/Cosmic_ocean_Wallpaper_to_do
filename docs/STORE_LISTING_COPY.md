# Play Store Listing Copy — Cosmic Ocean

## App Name
Cosmic Ocean

## Short Description (<= 80 chars)
Your lock screen becomes a calm, local-only task list.

## Full Description (suggested)
Cosmic Ocean turns your lock screen wallpaper into a calm, living task surface.  
Everything runs locally on your device — no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.

**Key Features**
- Local-first tasks and settings
- Real-time wallpaper updates
- Privacy controls for lock screen content
- Context-aware task highlights
- Focus sessions and due-time haptics
- Custom wallpapers with overlays
- Daily focus widget and quick actions

**Why it’s different**
Your task list stays with you, even offline.  
Cosmic Ocean is designed for clarity and calm — not notifications spam.
